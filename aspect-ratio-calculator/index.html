Frequently Asked Questions

What aspect ratio is 1920x1080?

1920x1080 has an aspect ratio of 16:9. This is the standard HD resolution used for most streaming platforms like Twitch and YouTube.

What is the best aspect ratio for streaming?

16:9 is the standard aspect ratio for streaming on Twitch, YouTube, and most platforms. For vertical content like TikTok or YouTube Shorts, use 9:16.

How do I calculate aspect ratio?

Divide the width by the height, then reduce to the simplest ratio. For example, 1920÷1080 = 1.778, which simplifies to 16:9.
