About OBS Mask Generator

We build free, high-performance browser tools for streamers. No downloads, no signups, just instant results.

Our Story

OBS Mask Generator started as a simple weekend project by a group of streaming engineers who were tired of opening Photoshop just to create a simple rounded border. We realized that thousands of other creators faced the same friction.

Today, we serve thousands of streamers daily, providing them with the essential utilities they need to make their broadcast look professional. From bitrate calculators to complex mask generation, our mission is to democratize high-quality streaming assets.

Why Trust Us?

Unlike SEO-farmed tool sites, our tools are built by people who actually stream. We understand the nuances of bitrate limits on Twitch, the frustration of OBS audio sync issues, and the importance of CPU-efficient browser sources.

  • ✅ Privacy First: All image processing happens locally in your browser. Your files never touch our servers.
  • ✅ Performance Focused: Our tools are lightweight and won't lag your stream.
  • ✅ Community Driven: We build what you ask for.
