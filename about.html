About Us

Our Mission

At OBS Mask Generator, our mission is simple: to help streamers and content creators enhance their visual presence without the need for complex software or expensive design skills. We believe that professional-looking streams should be accessible to everyone.

Why We Built This Tool

As streamers ourselves, we noticed a lack of simple, free tools for creating custom webcam shapes. Most solutions required downloading heavy image editing software just to make a simple rounded border or circle mask. We decided to change that by building a fast, browser-based tool that does one thing perfectly: generating OBS image masks.

Privacy & Performance

We prioritize your privacy and your computer's performance. Unlike many other online tools, OBS Mask Generator runs entirely in your browser. This means your images effectively never leave your device, and the processing happens locally, ensuring lightning-fast results without using up your bandwidth.

Ongoing Development

We are constantly improving our tools based on community feedback. From new shapes to additional utilities like bitrate calculators, we are dedicated to building a comprehensive suite of resources for the streaming community.
