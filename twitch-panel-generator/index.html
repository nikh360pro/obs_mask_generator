How to Create Perfect Twitch Panels

Your Twitch channel panels are the first thing viewers see when they visit your channel. A well-designed panel section can help you stand out, communicate important information, and build your brand. This free Twitch panel generator makes it easy to create professional-looking panels in seconds.

What Size Should Twitch Panels Be?

Twitch panels have a fixed width of 320 pixels. The height can range from 70px to 600px, but we recommend keeping panels between 70-150px for a clean, consistent look. Our generator automatically uses the correct dimensions.

Essential Panels Every Streamer Needs

  • About Me: Introduce yourself and your content
  • Schedule: When you go live
  • Donate/Support: Tips, subs, and support links
  • Discord: Community server invite
  • Rules: Chat guidelines
  • PC Specs: Your streaming setup

Can I Use Animated Panels?

Yes! Twitch supports animated GIF panels. Our generator includes animation effects like glow, shimmer, and gradient shifts. Just enable animation, choose your effect, and download as GIF.

How to Upload Panels to Twitch

  1. Create and download your panels using this tool
  2. Go to your Twitch channel page
  3. Click "Edit Panels" (below your stream)
  4. Click the + icon to add a new panel
  5. Upload your image and add a title/link
  6. Click "Submit" to save

Pro Tips for Panel Design

  • Keep text short and readable
  • Use consistent colors across all panels
  • Don't overuse animations - one or two animated panels is enough
  • Use icons to make panels scannable at a glance

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this Twitch panel generator really free?

Yes, completely free! No signup, no watermarks, no limits. Create as many panels as you want.

What file formats can I download?

PNG for static panels, GIF for animated panels. Both formats are supported by Twitch.

Can I upload my own images?

Currently we offer built-in icons and gradients. Custom image upload is coming soon!

Why is the width locked at 320px?

Twitch displays all panels at 320px width. Using a different width would cause stretching or squishing.
