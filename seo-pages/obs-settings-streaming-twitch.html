Recommended Twitch Settings 2024

  • Encoder: NVENC (New) for NVIDIA / x264 for CPU
  • Bitrate: 6000 Kbps (Twitch maximum)
  • Resolution: 1920×1080 or 1664×936
  • Framerate: 60 FPS
  • Keyframe Interval: 2 seconds
  • Rate Control: CBR
  • Preset: Quality or Max Quality

Twitch-Specific Tips

  • Always use CBR (Constant Bitrate) for Twitch
  • Set keyframe interval to exactly 2 seconds
  • Don't exceed 6000 Kbps - Twitch will reject it
  • Use your ingest server closest to you
  • Enable "Enforce streaming service encoder settings"

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best OBS settings for Twitch?

For Twitch: use NVENC encoder (NVIDIA) or x264, 6000 Kbps bitrate (Twitch max), 1080p60 or 936p60 resolution, CBR rate control, 2 second keyframe interval, and Quality preset.

What resolution should I stream at on Twitch?

1080p60 is ideal if you have the upload speed. 936p60 or 900p60 provides great quality at 6000 Kbps. For slower internet, 720p60 at 4500 Kbps works well.
