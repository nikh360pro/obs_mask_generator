Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best OBS settings for YouTube?

For YouTube: use NVENC encoder, 6000-12000 Kbps bitrate for 1080p60 (up to 51000 for 4K), 1080p or higher resolution, CBR rate control, and Max Quality preset.

What bitrate should I use for YouTube streaming?

YouTube allows higher bitrates than Twitch. For 1080p60, use 6000-12000 Kbps. For 1440p60, use 9000-18000 Kbps. For 4K60, use 20000-51000 Kbps.
