NVENC vs x264

  • CPU Impact: NVENC uses ~0% CPU, x264 uses 10-30%+
  • Quality: Modern NVENC (RTX 20/30/40) matches x264 slow
  • Best for: Gaming while streaming (NVENC), dedicated stream PC (x264)

Frequently Asked Questions

What NVENC preset should I use in OBS?

Use 'Quality' preset for the best balance. 'Max Quality' provides marginally better quality at higher GPU usage. 'Performance' is best for 4K or when GPU is struggling.

Should I use NVENC or x264?

Use NVENC if you have an NVIDIA GTX 1000 series or newer GPU. NVENC offloads encoding to the GPU, leaving your CPU free for gaming.
