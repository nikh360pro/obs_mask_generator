Recommended Settings for Low-End PCs

  • Resolution: 720p (1280×720)
  • Framerate: 30 FPS
  • Bitrate: 2500-3500 Kbps
  • Encoder: NVENC/AMF if available, otherwise x264
  • x264 Preset: Ultrafast or Superfast
  • Rate Control: CBR
  • Keyframe: 2 seconds

Performance Tips for Low-End PCs

  • Disable OBS preview while streaming
  • Use Game Capture instead of Display Capture
  • Close Chrome and other heavy apps
  • Lower in-game graphics settings
  • Disable Discord overlay and hardware acceleration
  • Consider streaming simpler games

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best OBS settings for a low-end PC?

For low-end PCs: use 720p 30fps resolution, 2500-3500 Kbps bitrate, x264 encoder with Ultrafast preset, and disable preview. If you have any GPU, use hardware encoding (NVENC/AMF) to reduce CPU load.

Can I stream with integrated graphics?

Yes, but with limitations. Use x264 encoder, lower resolution (720p or lower), low bitrate (2500-3000 Kbps), and close unnecessary programs. Intel Quick Sync can help on some integrated GPUs.
