YouTube 4K Bitrate Recommendations

  • 4K 60fps HDR: 44000-51000 Kbps
  • 4K 60fps SDR: 35000-45000 Kbps
  • 4K 30fps: 13000-34000 Kbps
  • Upload Speed Needed: 55-60+ Mbps

Requirements for 4K Streaming

4K streaming is demanding. Before attempting it, ensure you have:

  • Fiber internet with 50+ Mbps upload speed
  • RTX 3080/4080 or equivalent GPU for NVENC encoding
  • Powerful CPU (12+ cores) if using x264
  • 32GB+ RAM recommended
  • SSD storage for smooth capture

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best bitrate for YouTube 4K streaming?

For YouTube 4K 60fps streaming, use 20000-51000 Kbps. The recommended range is 35000-45000 Kbps for optimal quality without excessive file sizes. For 4K 30fps, 13000-34000 Kbps works well.

What upload speed do I need for 4K streaming?

For 4K streaming at 45000 Kbps, you need at least 55-60 Mbps upload speed for stable streaming with headroom. Most home internet connections cannot support 4K streaming.
