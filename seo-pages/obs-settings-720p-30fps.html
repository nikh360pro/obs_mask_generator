Recommended 720p 30fps Settings

For streamers with limited upload speed or older hardware, 720p 30fps provides a great balance:

  • Encoder: NVENC (preferred) or x264
  • Bitrate: 3000-4000 Kbps
  • Resolution: 1280x720 (Output)
  • Framerate: 30 FPS
  • Keyframe Interval: 2 seconds
  • Rate Control: CBR (Constant Bitrate)
  • Preset: Quality or Balanced

When to Use 720p 30fps

720p 30fps is perfect when:

  • Your upload speed is under 10 Mbps
  • You're streaming from a laptop or older PC
  • You're doing Just Chatting or low-motion content
  • You want to reduce CPU/GPU usage while gaming
  • Many viewers are also on mobile or slow connections

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best OBS settings for 720p 30fps?

For 720p 30fps streaming, use 3000-4000 Kbps bitrate, x264 or NVENC encoder, CBR rate control, and veryfast preset. This is ideal for slower internet connections or lower-end PCs.

What bitrate do I need for 720p 30fps?

For 720p 30fps, 3000-4000 Kbps is recommended. This requires about 5 Mbps upload speed for stable streaming. For low-motion content like Just Chatting, you can go as low as 2500 Kbps.
