How to Make a Circular Webcam in OBS

  1. Open OBS Mask Generator and click the "Circle" preset
  2. Adjust size to match your webcam resolution
  3. Download the mask PNG
  4. In OBS, right-click your webcam source → Filters
  5. Add "Image Mask/Blend" filter
  6. Browse to and select your circle mask

When to Use a Circle Webcam

  • Just Chatting: Adds a polished, professional look
  • Podcasts: Classic podcaster aesthetic
  • Mobile Games: Takes up less screen space
  • IRL Streams: Modern, clean appearance

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make my webcam circular in OBS?

Click the Circle preset on OBS Mask Generator, download the PNG, and apply it as an Image Mask filter to your webcam source in OBS Studio.

What size should my circle mask be?

Match your webcam resolution (usually 1920x1080 or 1280x720). The generator lets you set custom dimensions.
