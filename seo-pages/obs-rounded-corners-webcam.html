How to Add Rounded Corners in OBS

  1. Use OBS Mask Generator to create a rounded rectangle mask
  2. Download the mask as a PNG file
  3. In OBS, right-click your webcam source → Filters
  4. Click + and add "Image Mask/Blend" filter
  5. Browse to and select your downloaded mask
  6. Done! Your webcam now has rounded corners

Available Mask Shapes

  • Rounded Rectangle: Classic rounded corners, adjustable radius
  • Circle: Perfect circular webcam
  • Bubble: Square with heavy rounding
  • Hexagon: Modern geometric look
  • Custom Polygon: Any number of sides
