Tips for Choosing a Gamertag

  • Keep it under 15 characters for easy typing
  • Avoid hard-to-spell words
  • Check availability on all platforms first
  • Consider how it sounds when spoken on voice chat
  • Make it memorable and unique to you
  • Avoid using your real name or birth year

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I come up with a cool gaming name?

Combine words you like, add numbers or special characters, use name generators for inspiration, think about your favorite games or characters, and keep it short and memorable. Our AI generator can create hundreds of unique options instantly.

What makes a good gamertag?

A good gamertag is: memorable, easy to pronounce, available on your preferred platforms, not offensive, and reflects your personality or gaming style. Avoid excessive numbers or random characters.
