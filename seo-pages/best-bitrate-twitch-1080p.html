Twitch 1080p Bitrate Recommendations

  • 1080p 60fps Gaming: 6000 Kbps (Twitch max)
  • 1080p 30fps Gaming: 4500-5000 Kbps
  • 1080p 60fps Just Chatting: 4500-5000 Kbps
  • 1080p 30fps Low Motion: 3500-4000 Kbps

Why 6000 Kbps for Twitch?

Twitch caps bitrate at 6000 Kbps for several reasons:

  • Ensures viewers on mobile/slow connections can watch
  • Provides transcoding options for non-partners
  • Balances quality with accessibility
  • Higher bitrates don't improve quality significantly

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best bitrate for Twitch 1080p?

For Twitch 1080p streaming, use 6000 Kbps (Twitch's maximum allowed bitrate). For 1080p 60fps, this is the recommended setting. For 1080p 30fps, 4500-5000 Kbps works well.

What is Twitch's maximum bitrate?

Twitch's maximum allowed bitrate is 6000 Kbps. Going higher won't improve quality and may cause issues. For best results, ensure your upload speed is at least 8-10 Mbps.
