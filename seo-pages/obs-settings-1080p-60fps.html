Recommended 1080p 60fps Settings

For most streamers targeting 1080p at 60 frames per second, here are the baseline settings:

  • Encoder: NVENC (NVIDIA) or x264 (CPU)
  • Bitrate: 6000 Kbps (Twitch) / 8000-12000 Kbps (YouTube)
  • Resolution: 1920x1080 (Output)
  • Framerate: 60 FPS
  • Keyframe Interval: 2 seconds
  • Rate Control: CBR (Constant Bitrate)

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best OBS settings for 1080p 60fps?

For 1080p 60fps streaming, use NVENC encoder (if NVIDIA GPU), 6000 Kbps bitrate for Twitch (up to 12000 for YouTube), CBR rate control, and 2 second keyframe interval. Our free generator calculates optimal settings for your specific hardware.

What bitrate do I need for 1080p 60fps?

For 1080p 60fps, Twitch recommends 6000 Kbps (their maximum). YouTube allows up to 12000 Kbps for better quality. Ensure your upload speed is at least 20% higher than your bitrate.
