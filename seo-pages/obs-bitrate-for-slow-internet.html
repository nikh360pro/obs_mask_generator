Recommended Settings by Upload Speed

  • 5 Mbps: 720p 30fps at 2500-3000 Kbps
  • 7 Mbps: 720p 30fps at 3500-4000 Kbps
  • 10 Mbps: 720p 60fps at 4000-5000 Kbps
  • 15 Mbps: 900p 60fps at 5000-6000 Kbps

Tips for Streaming on Slow Internet

  • Use ethernet instead of WiFi
  • Close background apps (Discord, Chrome, etc.)
  • Stream during off-peak hours
  • Lower in-game graphics settings
  • Consider streaming at 30fps instead of 60fps
  • Use "Dynamic Bitrate" in OBS Advanced settings

Frequently Asked Questions

What bitrate should I use with slow internet?

For 5 Mbps upload: use 2500-3000 Kbps at 720p30. For 10 Mbps upload: use 4000-5000 Kbps at 720p60 or 900p30. Always leave 20-30% headroom.

Can I stream with 5 Mbps upload?

Yes, but you'll need to use lower settings. Stream at 720p 30fps with 2500-3000 Kbps bitrate. Use ethernet if possible.
