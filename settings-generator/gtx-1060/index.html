Best OBS Settings for NVIDIA GTX 1060

Quick Answer: The GTX 1060 uses the "Pascal" encoder. It's decent, but struggles at 1080p fast motion. Stick to 720p 60fps at 4,500 Kbps for the best balance.

3GB vs 6GB Version

  • 6GB Version: Can handle 1080p 30fps or 720p 60fps easily.
  • 3GB Version: You are VRAM limited. Use "Performance" preset. Do NOT use "Max Quality" or "Look-ahead".
