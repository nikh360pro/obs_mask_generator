🎮 Quick Answer — Gothic 1 Remake Streaming

Use NVENC H.264 encoder, 8000 Kbps bitrate, 1080p 60fps, P5 preset, and CBR rate control. Because this is a dense RPG, higher bitrates are needed to prevent blurring when moving through forests or combat scenes.

Why High Bitrate is Required

RPGs like Gothic 1 Remake with lush forests cause what is known as "generation loss." The encoder struggles to track thousands of moving leaves on screen. By pushing your bitrate to Twitch's soft cap of 8000 Kbps, you ensure your stream remains crisp.
