Best OBS Settings for Call of Duty: Warzone

Quick Answer: Warzone is extremely CPU intensive. You MUST use NVENC (GPU) encoding. Do not use x264. Set bitrate to 6,000 Kbps (Twitch limit) or higher for YouTube.

The 936p Secret

Warzone moves so fast that 6,000 Kbps isn't enough for 1080p. The image breaks up. Many pros stream at 1664x936. It looks sharper because it fits the bitrate better.
