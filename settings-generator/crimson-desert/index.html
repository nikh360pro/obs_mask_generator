Best OBS Settings for Crimson Desert (2026)

Updated June 2026 • Action RPG Guide

🎮 Quick Answer — Crimson Desert Streaming

Use NVENC H.264 encoder, 8000 Kbps bitrate, 1080p 60fps, P5 preset, and CBR rate control. Fast-paced combat and lots of particle effects require high bitrate and a fast encoder preset to avoid dropped frames.

Recommended OBS Settings for Crimson Desert

Crimson Desert is graphically intense and features very fast camera movements. To prevent your stream from looking like pixelated soup during combat, use these settings.
