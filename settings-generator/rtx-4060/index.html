Why These Settings Work for RTX 4060

The NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4060 features the 8th generation NVENC encoder based on the Ada Lovelace architecture. This is the same encoder found in the RTX 4070, 4080, and 4090, meaning you get flagship-level encoding quality at a mid-range price.

The key advantage of the RTX 4060 for streaming is hardware AV1 encoding. AV1 provides approximately 40% better quality at the same bitrate compared to H.264. If your platform supports AV1 (YouTube does, Twitch has beta support), you can achieve crisp 1080p streams at just 4500-5000 Kbps instead of the usual 6000 Kbps.

The P6 preset is recommended because it offers the best balance of quality and performance. While P7 provides marginally better quality, the RTX 4060 can handle P6 without any impact on gaming performance, and the visual difference is negligible at Twitch's 6000 Kbps cap.

Common Issues with RTX 4060 Streaming

  • 8GB VRAM Limitation: The RTX 4060 has 8GB of VRAM, which can be limiting when streaming graphically demanding games at high settings. Consider lowering in-game texture quality or streaming resolution if you experience stuttering.
  • Single Encoder: Unlike the RTX 4070 and above, the RTX 4060 has a single NVENC encoder. This means you cannot run multiple simultaneous encodes (e.g., streaming to two platforms at different settings) without impacting performance.
  • AV1 Platform Support: While the RTX 4060 supports AV1 encoding, not all platforms accept AV1 streams yet. Twitch's AV1 support is still in beta, so H.264 remains the safer choice for Twitch streaming.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is RTX 4060 good for streaming?

Yes, the RTX 4060 is excellent for 1080p streaming. It features the 8th generation NVENC encoder with hardware AV1 support, allowing high-quality streams with minimal impact on gaming performance. It's one of the best budget-friendly GPUs for content creators in 2026.

Should I use NVENC or x264 with RTX 4060?

Always use NVENC with RTX 4060. The 8th gen NVENC encoder offers quality comparable to x264 Medium preset while using almost no CPU resources, leaving your CPU free for gaming. There's no scenario where x264 makes sense on an RTX 4060 system for streaming.

What bitrate should I use with RTX 4060 for Twitch?

For Twitch streaming at 1080p 60fps with RTX 4060, use 6000 Kbps (Twitch's maximum). For 720p 60fps, 4500-5000 Kbps is ideal. If you have AV1 access, you can achieve similar quality at 4000-5000 Kbps.
