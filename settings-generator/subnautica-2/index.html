Why Subnautica 2 Is Hard to Stream (UE5 Explained)

Unlike previous Subnautica titles, Subnautica 2 uses Unreal Engine 5 with Lumen and Nanite enabled. Here is why this makes streaming harder:

  • Lumen Global Illumination — Dynamic real-time lighting is extremely GPU-intensive. It can spike GPU usage to 95-100%, leaving no headroom for OBS.
  • Nanite Geometry — Millions of micro-polygons for detailed rock formations and coral stress your VRAM bandwidth.
  • Shader Compilation Stutter — On first launch or after driver updates, UE5 games stutter while compiling shaders. This is normal and resolves after 5-10 minutes.
  • Texture Streaming — Biome transitions can cause VRAM spikes, especially on 8GB GPUs.

⚠️ If You Are Getting Dropped Frames or Stutter

This is almost always caused by GPU overload. Fix it by: (1) enabling DLSS Quality or FSR Quality in-game, (2) lowering Global Illumination to Medium or Low, (3) reducing Shadow Quality, and (4) capping your in-game FPS to 120 via the game's FPS limiter.

FAQ — Subnautica 2 OBS Streaming

What are the best OBS settings for Subnautica 2?

NVENC H.264 encoder, 6000–8000 Kbps, 1080p 60fps, P5 preset, CBR rate control. Enable DLSS Quality in-game to free up GPU resources for OBS encoding.

Why does Subnautica 2 stutter when I stream?

Unreal Engine 5's Lumen lighting and Nanite geometry push GPUs hard. Enable DLSS/FSR Quality mode, lower Global Illumination to Medium, and cap your in-game FPS at 120. If stutter persists after the first 10 minutes of gameplay, it is likely shader compilation — it will stop on its own.

Should I use NVENC or x264 for Subnautica 2?

Always use NVENC (or AMF for AMD GPUs) for Subnautica 2. The game uses so much GPU already that x264 CPU encoding adds additional load and will cause dropped frames or stuttering. Hardware encoding offloads the video compression to a dedicated chip on your GPU, leaving your CPU and GPU free for the game.

Can I stream Subnautica 2 on a low-end PC?

If you have a GTX 1660 Super or lower, stream at 720p 60fps instead of 1080p. Use FSR Quality mode in-game, set all graphics to Medium, and use NVENC Performance preset in OBS. The game is demanding but manageable at reduced quality settings.

What bitrate should I use for Subnautica 2 on Twitch?

Use 6000–8000 Kbps on Twitch. Subnautica 2's dark underwater caves and particle effects compress poorly at low bitrates, causing visible artifacts. Higher bitrate = cleaner stream in complex scenes.
