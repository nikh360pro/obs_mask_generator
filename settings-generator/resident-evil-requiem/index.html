Best OBS Settings for Resident Evil Requiem (2026)

Updated June 2026 • Horror Game Guide

🎮 Quick Answer — RE: Requiem Streaming

Use NVENC H.264 encoder, 8000 Kbps bitrate, 1080p 60fps, P6 preset, and CBR rate control. Horror games are notoriously difficult to stream because dark areas suffer from severe compression banding. High bitrate is a must.

Recommended OBS Settings for RE: Requiem

Streaming dark horror games requires careful tuning. If your bitrate is too low, the shadows will become blocky and pixelated, ruining the immersion for your viewers.
