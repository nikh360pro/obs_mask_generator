Why Valorant is Easy to Stream

Valorant is well-optimized and runs at high FPS even on modest hardware. Key advantages:

  • Low GPU usage - Leaves headroom for NVENC encoding
  • Low CPU usage - x264 encoding is viable if no GPU encoder
  • Consistent frame times - Smooth streaming experience

In-Game Settings Tips

  • Cap FPS at ~300 to reduce system load
  • Multithreaded Rendering: ON
  • Material Quality: Low (minimal visual difference)

Yes, even mid-range GPUs (RTX 3060, GTX 1660) handle Valorant streaming smoothly.
