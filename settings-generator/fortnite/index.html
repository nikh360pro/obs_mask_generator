Why These Settings for Fortnite

Fortnite is a fast-paced battle royale with rapid building, editing, and combat. The game's visual complexity requires:

  • 60fps minimum - 30fps looks choppy during build fights
  • 6000 Kbps - Maximum Twitch bitrate for clarity during chaotic fights
  • 1080p - Lower resolutions lose detail on distant players

In-Game Settings for Streaming

  • Cap FPS at 144-240 (reduces GPU load)
  • Performance Mode if needed for stable FPS
  • Disable replays while streaming

FAQ

Best OBS settings for Fortnite?

NVENC encoder, 6000 Kbps, 1080p 60fps. Use P5 preset for quality.

What resolution for Fortnite streaming?

1080p for best quality. Some pros use 936p for slightly better bitrate efficiency.
