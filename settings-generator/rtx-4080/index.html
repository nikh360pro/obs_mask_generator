Why These Settings Work for RTX 4080

The RTX 4080 features dual 8th generation NVENC encoders - the same as the RTX 4090. This means identical encoding quality at a lower price point. The 16GB VRAM handles any game while streaming.

For most streamers, the RTX 4080 is the "sweet spot" for high-end content creation. It can handle 1440p streaming with AV1 while gaming at 4K, or run simultaneous encodes for multi-platform streaming.

Common Issues with RTX 4080 Streaming

  • Price vs Value: The RTX 4070 Ti offers nearly identical streaming quality for significantly less. Only choose the 4080 if you need the extra gaming/rendering performance.
  • 16GB VRAM: More than enough for streaming, but the RTX 4090's 24GB is better for heavy video editing + streaming workflows.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is RTX 4080 good for streaming?

Excellent. Dual NVENC encoders with AV1 support make it one of the best streaming GPUs available.

RTX 4080 vs 4090 for streaming?

Same encoder quality. The 4090 adds gaming headroom and 24GB VRAM, but for pure streaming, the 4080 is the smarter buy.

What bitrate for RTX 4080 1440p?

For 1440p 60fps on YouTube with AV1, use 9000-12000 Kbps. Twitch caps at 6000 Kbps, so stream at 1080p for best quality there.
