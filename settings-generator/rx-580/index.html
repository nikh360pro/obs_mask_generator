Best OBS Settings for AMD RX 580

Quick Answer: The AMF encoder on the RX 580 is not great at low bitrates. To avoid "blocky" quality, you need to use YouTube (where you can push 10,000+ Kbps) or switch to x264 (CPU) if your CPU is strong (Ryzen 5 3600 or better).

The AMF Problem

The older H.264 engine on Polaris cards (RX 400/500) struggles with fast motion on Twitch's 6000 Kbps limit.

Workaround: High Bitrate (YouTube/Kick)

If you stream on YouTube, quality is easy.

  • Bitrate: 15,000 Kbps
  • Resolution: 1440p (Upscaled from 1080p). YouTube gives better codec (VP9) to 1440p streams.

Recommended Settings (Twitch)

If you MUST stream to Twitch with an RX 580:
