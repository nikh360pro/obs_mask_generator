Frequently Asked Questions

What are Twitch Channel Points?

Channel Points are a customizable loyalty feature on Twitch. Viewers earn points by watching streams and can redeem them for rewards you create.

How do I add these rewards to my Twitch channel?

Go to your Twitch Creator Dashboard ‚Üí Viewer Rewards ‚Üí Channel Points ‚Üí Manage Rewards & Challenges ‚Üí Add New Reward.

How many points should I charge?

It depends on the reward difficulty. Simple rewards: 100-500 points. Medium: 1,000-5,000 points. Epic rewards: 10,000+ points.
