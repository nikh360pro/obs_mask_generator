Pre-Stream Webcam Checklist

  1. Camera is detected - Can OBS see your webcam?
  2. Right camera selected - Not your laptop camera by accident
  3. Good lighting - Face lit from front, not back
  4. Focus is sharp - Clean lens, auto-focus enabled
  5. Framing is good - Head and shoulders visible
  6. Background looks okay - Nothing embarrassing visible

Common Webcam Issues

  • Camera not detected - Unplug/replug USB, restart OBS
  • Dark/grainy image - Add more lighting (ring light helps)
  • Webcam in use by another app - Close Zoom, Discord, etc.
  • Low frame rate - USB 3.0 port, close other apps

Webcam Settings for Streaming

  • Resolution: 720p or 1080p
  • Frame rate: 30 or 60 FPS
  • Auto-focus: Off (set manual focus if possible)
  • Auto-exposure: Off for consistent lighting

FAQ

Why doesn't my webcam work in the browser?

Make sure you've granted camera permissions. Look for the camera icon in your browser's address bar.

How do I fix a blurry webcam?

Clean the lens, improve lighting, and if your camera supports it, manually set focus instead of using auto-focus.
