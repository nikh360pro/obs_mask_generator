Frequently Asked Questions

Can I remove backgrounds from Zoom recordings made in gallery view?

Yes, but it works best with speaker view recordings (one person at a time). For gallery view, consider extracting individual speakers first using a video editor, then process each separately.

Does this work with screen share portions of Zoom recordings?

No - background removal only works with camera footage showing people. Trim out screen sharing segments before processing, or keep those parts as-is in your final edit.

What if my Zoom recording has poor lighting?

The AI works best with good lighting, but can handle moderate low-light situations. For very dark videos, try increasing brightness in a video editor before processing. Future tip: Use a ring light or face a window during recording.

Can I use this for Zoom webinars with multiple panelists?

Yes! The AI can detect and remove backgrounds for multiple people. However, for best results, ensure all panelists are well-lit and clearly visible. Panel discussions with 2-4 people work great.

How long does processing take for a typical webinar?

Processing time depends on video length. A 5-minute segment takes about 1-2 minutes. For hour-long webinars, consider breaking into 5-10 minute chapters and processing separately for faster results.

Can I add my company branding as the background?

Absolutely! After downloading your background-removed video, import it into any video editor (DaVinci Resolve, CapCut, Premiere Pro) and add your branded background image or video underneath. This is perfect for repurposing webinars into marketing content.
