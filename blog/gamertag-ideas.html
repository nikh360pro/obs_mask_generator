What Makes a Good Gamertag?

  • Memorable - Easy to remember and recognize
  • Short - 3-15 characters ideal
  • Unique - Available on all platforms
  • Easy to spell - No confusing numbers or symbols
  • Future-proof - Won't be embarrassing in 5 years

Gamertag Formulas

  • Adjective + Animal - ShadowWolf, SwiftHawk
  • Color + Object - RedStorm, BlueFrost
  • Action + Noun - NightRider, StormBreaker
  • Mythical Reference - Phoenix, Titan, Raven
  • Initials + Numbers - JD_Gaming, XK47
