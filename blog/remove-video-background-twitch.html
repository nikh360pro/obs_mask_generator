Step-by-Step: Remove Background for Twitch Clips

Step 1: Record Your Clip

You have two options:

  • Option A: Record directly in OBS (File ‚Üí Recording ‚Üí Start Recording). Record your webcam-only scene.
  • Option B: Use Twitch's clip feature to save a highlight from a past stream.

Best Practices for Recording:

  • Use your ring light or face a window (lighting improves AI accuracy)
  • Wear clothes that contrast with your background
  • Keep it short (10-15 seconds for fastest processing)
  • Avoid extreme motion blur (normal gaming reactions are fine)

Step 2: Upload to Background Remover

Go to the Video Background Remover. Drag your clip (MP4 or MOV) into the upload zone. Files up to 30MB supported (most clips are 5-20MB).

Step 3: Select Yourself (One Click)

When your video loads, you'll see the first frame. Click once on yourself (or the subject you want to keep). The AI will instantly create a mask around you.

  • Green Dot: Keep this area (you + headset/mic/chair)
  • Red Dot (Right Click): Remove this area (background clutter)

Pro tip: If the AI includes your desk or gaming chair and you don't want it, right-click those areas to exclude them.

Step 4: Process Video

Click "Process Video." Our AI (SAM 2.1 + BiRefNet) will track you across all frames and remove the background. Processing takes 1-2 minutes for a 10-second clip.

Step 5: Download & Import to OBS

Download your processed video. You'll get an MP4 with a transparent background (or green screen background for compatibility).

Import to OBS Studio:

  1. Open OBS Studio
  2. Add new source ‚Üí Media Source
  3. Browse and select your downloaded video
  4. Check "Loop" if you want it to repeat
  5. (Optional) If your video has a green background, add a "Chroma Key" filter to the Media Source and select green
  6. Position and resize in your scene

Not anymore! This tool lets you create transparent background videos without a green screen. However, for live webcam overlays (not pre-recorded), you'll still need a green screen or use OBS's "Background Removal" plugin.

Can I use this for live streaming?

This tool is for pre-recorded clips (intros, outros, alerts, highlight reels). For live webcam background removal, use OBS's "Background Removal" plugin (powered by similar AI).
