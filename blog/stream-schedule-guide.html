Why Have a Stream Schedule?

  • Builds audience habits - Viewers know when to return
  • Improves discoverability - Twitch shows scheduled streams
  • Shows professionalism - Makes you look more serious
  • Helps your routine - Structure keeps you consistent

Where to Display Your Schedule

  1. Twitch Schedule Panel - Below your stream (320px wide)
  2. Twitch Schedule Feature - Built-in calendar in Creator Dashboard
  3. Twitter/X Bio - Quick mention of stream days
  4. Discord Server - Dedicated schedule channel
  5. Offline Banner - Show next stream time

Schedule Tips for New Streamers

  • Start small - 2-3 days per week is fine
  • Be consistent - Same days, same times every week
  • Include timezone - Your viewers may be worldwide
  • Leave buffer room - Don't schedule back-to-back with work

Using Twitch's Built-in Schedule

  1. Go to Creator Dashboard â†’ Schedule
  2. Click + Add Stream
  3. Set date, time, game category, and title
  4. Set as recurring for weekly streams
  5. Twitch will notify followers before scheduled streams

FAQ

How often should I stream?

For growth, 3-5 times per week for 3-4 hours each. Quality over quantity - don't burn out.

What if I miss a scheduled stream?

Update your schedule and notify followers on social media. Consistency matters, but life happens.
