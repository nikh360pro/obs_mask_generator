How to Remove Instagram Reels Background (Step-by-Step)

Step 1: Record Your Reel (Best Practices)

Before removing the background, film your Reel with these tips for best results:

  • Lighting: Natural light (face a window) or a ring light prevents shadow artifacts
  • Contrast: Wear colors that contrast with your background (makes AI segmentation easier)
  • Vertical Format: Film in 9:16 ratio for native Reels format
  • Moderate Movement: Normal gestures work great; extreme motion blur can cause edge issues
  • Length: 15-30 seconds is ideal (faster processing + higher watch time)

Save your Reel to camera roll before uploading to Instagram.

Step 2: Upload to Background Remover

Go to the Video Background Remover and drag your Reel (MP4 or MOV) into the upload zone. Files up to 30MB are supported (most Reels are 10-20MB).

Step 3: Select Yourself (One Click)

Once the video loads, you'll see the first frame. Click once on yourself (or the product you're showcasing). The AI instantly visualizes a mask around you.

  • Green Dot: Keep this area (you)
  • Red Dot (Right Click): Remove this area (background objects)

Pro tip: If you're holding a product or prop, the AI automatically includes it. Just click on yourself.

Step 4: Process Video

Click "Process Video." Our AI (SAM 2.1 technology) analyzes every frame to separate you from the background. Typical 20-second Reel processes in 1-2 minutes.

Step 5: Download & Edit in Instagram

Download your processed video. You'll get a version with a transparent background (or green screen background for compatibility). Now import it into Instagram:

Method A: Direct Instagram Upload

  1. Open Instagram and tap the + button
  2. Select "Reel"
  3. Upload your processed video
  4. Use Instagram's "Layout" or "Green Screen" effect to add a custom background
  5. Post as Reel

Method B: Advanced Editing (CapCut or InShot)

  1. Import processed video into CapCut or InShot
  2. Use "Remove BG" or "Chroma Key" to remove green background
  3. Add your custom background (video, image, gradient, animation)
  4. Add text, music, effects
  5. Export and upload to Instagram as Reel
