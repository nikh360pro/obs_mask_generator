What is a Camera Mask?

A camera mask is an image that hides parts of your webcam. It's how streamers get:

  • Rounded corner webcams
  • Circular facecams
  • Custom shapes (hexagon, star, etc.)
  • Decorative borders and frames

Method 1: Image Mask (Recommended)

  1. Create or download a mask image (black = visible, white = hidden)
  2. In OBS, right-click your webcam source
  3. Go to Filters ‚Üí + ‚Üí Image Mask/Blend
  4. Select your mask image
  5. Type: Alpha Mask (Alpha Channel)

üí° Mask Image Rules

Black areas stay visible, white areas become transparent. Use PNG format with transparency for best results.

Method 2: Overlay Frame

  1. Create a PNG frame with transparent center
  2. Add as Image source in OBS
  3. Position it over your webcam

This method adds decoration but doesn't crop the webcam itself.

FAQ

Does a camera mask affect performance?

No, image masks use minimal resources. It's just hiding pixels.

Can I animate the mask?

Yes! Use a video file instead of an image for animated borders.
