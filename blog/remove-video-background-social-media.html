Frequently Asked Questions

Which social media platform should I prioritize for video content?

B2B/Professionals: LinkedIn (highest ROI for career/business growth). B2C/Lifestyle: Instagram + Facebook. Tech/News: Twitter/X. All: Start where your audience is most active.

Can I post the same video across all platforms?

Yes, but optimize for each: LinkedIn = professional tone, Facebook = casual, Twitter = punchy, Instagram = aesthetic. Adjust background, captions, and aspect ratio per platform for best results.

How often should I post video content?

LinkedIn: 2-3x per week. Facebook: Daily (if business page). Twitter: 3-5x per week. Instagram: Feed 3-4x per week, Reels daily. Consistency > frequency.

Do I need different backgrounds for personal brand vs business?

Personal brand: Can use gradients, lifestyle scenes, personality-driven. Business: Stick to company branding colors/templates for consistency. Either way, clean backgrounds signal professionalism.

What's the ideal video length for social media?

LinkedIn: 30-90 seconds. Facebook: 15-60 seconds. Twitter: 6-15 seconds. Instagram Feed: 26 seconds. Shorter = higher completion rate = better algorithm boost.

Can I run paid ads with videos created using this tool?

Absolutely! Remove background, add branded backgrounds, export in ad specs (usually 1:1 or 4:5), upload to Ads Manager. Works for Facebook Ads, LinkedIn Ads, Twitter Ads, Instagram Ads.
