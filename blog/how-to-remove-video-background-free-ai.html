Why Using a Green Screen is "Old School"

Traditional green screens (Chroma Key) rely on color difference. If your shirt is green, you disappear. If your lighting is uneven, you get "fuzz."

AI Rotoscoping is different. It "sees" the person, not the color. This means:

  • You can wear any color clothes.
  • You don't need professional lighting.
  • You can have a cluttered bedroom background.
