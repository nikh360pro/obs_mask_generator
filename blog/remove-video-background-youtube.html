Step-by-Step: Remove YouTube Video Background

Step 1: Record Your Footage

Record your video using your camera or webcam. Best practices:

  • Lighting: Face a window or use a ring light (prevents shadows)
  • Contrast: Wear clothes that differ from your background color
  • Frame Rate: 24fps or 30fps (standard for YouTube)
  • Resolution: 1080p minimum (balances quality and file size)
  • Length: For Shorts, keep it 10-15 seconds. For longer videos, process in segments.

Step 2: Upload to Background Remover

Visit the Video Background Remover. Drag and drop your video (MP4, MOV, or WEBM). Files up to 30MB supported.

Step 3: Select Subject

Click on yourself (or the subject) in the first frame. The AI instantly creates a mask around you.

  • Green Dot: Keep this area (you + any props)
  • Red Dot (Right Click): Remove this area (background)

Pro tip: For product reviews, click on both yourself AND the product to keep both in frame.

Step 4: Process Video

Click "Process Video." AI analyzes every frame using SAM 2.1 technology. Processing takes 1-2 minutes for a 10-second clip.

Step 5: Download & Edit

Download your transparent background video. Now import it into your video editor:

Premiere Pro

  1. Import processed video to timeline
  2. Place above your background layer (image, video, or solid color)
  3. Video automatically composites with alpha channel

DaVinci Resolve

  1. Import video to Media Pool
  2. Drag to timeline above background
  3. Use Fusion page for advanced compositing (optional)

CapCut (Free & Beginner-Friendly)

  1. Import processed video
  2. Use "Chroma Key" effect (select green if needed)
  3. Add custom background layer below
  4. Export for YouTube

YouTube compresses all uploads. To maintain quality, export at the highest resolution possible (1080p or 4K) and use H.264 codec.
