Frequently Asked Questions

What size should a Twitch schedule panel be?

Twitch schedule panels should be 320 pixels wide. Height can vary based on content. This generator creates 320×400 images perfect for Twitch panels.

How do I show my stream schedule on Twitch?

Add a panel to your channel with your schedule image, use Twitch's built-in Schedule feature, or add schedule info to your About section.
