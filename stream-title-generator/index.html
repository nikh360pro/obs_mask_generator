Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a good stream title?

A good stream title is short (under 100 characters), includes the game name or activity, has an engaging hook, and optionally uses emojis. It should give viewers a reason to click.

Do stream titles affect discoverability?

Yes! Stream titles help with Twitch and YouTube search. Include relevant keywords like the game name, activity type, or trending topics to improve discoverability.

Should I use emojis in my stream title?

Emojis can make your title stand out in the browse page. Use 1-2 relevant emojis at the start or end of your title. Don't overdo it - too many emojis look spammy.
