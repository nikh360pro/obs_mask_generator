How to Create a Professional Twitch Stream Schedule

Consistency is the #1 rule of growing on Twitch. If viewers don't know when you will be online, they can't make time for you. A visual schedule panel on your profile is a commitment to your audience.

What to Include in Your Schedule

  • Days & Times: Be specific. "Mon/Wed/Fri at 7 PM EST".
  • Timezone: Always include your timezone (EST, PST, GMT, CET). Your viewers are global!
  • Content Type: "Minecraft Mondays", "Horror Fridays". Give them a reason to tune in for specific days.

Where to Post Your Schedule

Don't just hide it in your Twitch panels. Post this image on:

  • Twitter/X: Pin it to your profile.
  • Discord: Post it in your #announcements channel every Sunday.
  • Twitch Offline Screen: Use it as your offline banner so people see it when you aren't live.

How to Use This Generator

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I put this schedule on Twitch?

Go to your Twitch channel profile > click "Edit Panels" > click "+" > "Add Text or Image Panel". Upload the image you downloaded from this tool, and link it to your Twitter or Discord.

What is the best size for a Twitch schedule?

Twitch panels are 320px wide. Our generator automatically creates images at 320px width so they fit perfectly without becoming blurry or resized.
