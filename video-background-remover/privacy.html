Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy is GDPR and CCPA compliant.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information when you use our service:

  • Account Information: Email address, user ID (provided by Firebase Authentication/Google)
  • Usage Data: Daily video processing count, last processing date
  • Authentication Data: Google Sign-In credentials (managed by Firebase)

1.2 Automatically Collected Data

  • IP Address: For security, rate limiting, and audit logging
  • Browser Information: User agent, browser type (via Google Analytics)
  • Analytics Data: Page views, session duration (via Google Analytics 4, ID: G-C84663S6K3) - only collected after cookie consent

1.3 Content You Upload

  • Video Files: Temporarily stored for processing
  • Click Points: Coordinates you select on video frames for object segmentation

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Legal Basis (GDPR Article 6)

  • Contract Performance: Processing videos as part of our service
  • Legitimate Interest: Enforcing daily quotas, preventing abuse
  • Consent: Analytics tracking (you can withdraw consent anytime)

2.2 Purposes

  • Process your uploaded videos to remove backgrounds
  • Enforce daily usage quotas (2 videos per day for free users)
  • Improve our service through analytics (with your consent)
  • Prevent abuse and ensure security

3. Data Retention Periods

Data Type Retention Period
Uploaded Videos (Input) Deleted immediately after processing
Processed Videos (Output) Available for download until you close the session
Quota Data (Firestore) 90 days from last activity
Audit Logs 1 year (security/compliance)
Google Analytics Data 14 months (Google's default)

4. Your Rights (GDPR Articles 15-22)

4.1 Right to Access (Article 15)

You can export all your personal data:

  • API Endpoint: GET /api/user/export (requires login)
  • What you get: JSON file with all stored data (email, quota info, Firestore data)

4.2 Right to Erasure / "Right to be Forgotten" (Article 17)

You can delete your account and all associated data:

  • API Endpoint: DELETE /api/user/account (requires login)
  • What gets deleted: Firebase Authentication account, Firestore quota data, all personal information
  • Irreversible: This action cannot be undone

4.3 Right to Data Portability (Article 20)

You can receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format (JSON) via the export API.

4.4 Right to Object (Article 21)

You can object to analytics tracking by rejecting cookies or using our cookie banner to manage preferences.

4.5 Right to Rectification (Article 16)

Contact us at obsmaskgenerator@gmail.com to update incorrect personal information.

5. How We Handle Your Videos

We take your privacy seriously. Videos uploaded to our service are:

  • Processed locally: Video processing happens on our secure servers (not sent to third-party AI services)
  • Immediately deleted: Input videos are deleted automatically after processing completes
  • Not used for training: Your videos are NEVER used to train AI models or shared with third parties
  • Temporary storage: During processing, videos are stored in RAM (/dev/shm) for performance and automatic cleanup
  • No cloud backup: We don't create backups of your uploaded videos
