Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use this for live streaming?

This tool processes pre-recorded clips (not real-time). Use it for intro videos, highlight reels, or TikTok/YouTube Shorts. For live webcam overlay, you'd need a green screen + OBS Chroma Key filter.

How long can my clips be?

Maximum 10 seconds per clip. For longer content (like full intro videos), split into 10-second segments, process separately, then stitch together in a free editor like DaVinci Resolve or CapCut.

Will this work with my gaming chair / LED lights?

Yes! The AI automatically detects you and removes everything else - gaming chairs, LED strips, monitors, posters, etc. Only you remain in the final video.

Can I create sub alerts / donation alerts with this?
