Frequently Asked Questions

Is Obsmaskgenerator free to use?

Yes! We offer a free tier that allows you to process up to 2 videos per day.

What video formats do you support?

We support most common video formats including MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, and WebM.

Why is there a 10-second limit?

To ensure fast processing times for all users on our GPU servers, we currently limit video length to 10 seconds. We encourage you to trim your clips before uploading.

How accurate is the background removal?

We use Meta's state-of-the-art SAM2 model, which provides industry-leading accuracy. You can refine the selection by adding "Keep" and "Remove" points.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. Your videos are processed purely for the background removal task and are automatically deleted from our servers afterwards.
