Last updated: February 3, 2026

This Acceptable Use Policy governs your use of the Video Background Remover service.

1. Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy outlines prohibited uses of our Video Background Remover service. By using our service, you agree to comply with these rules. Violations may result in account suspension or termination.

2. Age Restrictions

2.1 Minimum Age Requirements

  • United States: You must be at least 13 years old (COPPA compliance)
  • European Union: You must be at least 16 years old (GDPR Article 8)
  • Other Jurisdictions: You must be at least 18 years old, or the age of majority in your jurisdiction

2.2 Parental Consent

If you are between 13-17 years old (US) or 16-17 years old (EU), you may only use this service with verifiable parental or guardian consent.

3. Prohibited Content

You may NOT upload, process, or distribute videos containing:

3.1 Illegal Content

  • Content that violates any local, state, national, or international law
  • Content that infringes on intellectual property rights (copyright, trademark, patent)
  • Content depicting illegal activities (drug use, violence, terrorism)
  • Content that violates export control laws

3.2 Harmful Content

  • Child Exploitation: Any content involving minors in sexually suggestive situations (zero tolerance - immediate account deletion and law enforcement notification)
  • Non-Consensual Content: Videos recorded or distributed without the subject's knowledge or consent
  • Revenge Porn: Intimate images shared without consent
  • Harassment: Content intended to harass, bully, or threaten individuals

3.3 Hateful Content

  • Content promoting hate speech, discrimination, or violence based on:
    • Race, ethnicity, national origin
    • Religion or religious affiliation
    • Gender, gender identity, or sexual orientation
    • Disability or medical condition
    • Age or veteran status

3.4 Deceptive Content

  • Deepfakes: Synthetic media created to deceive or mislead (especially political deepfakes or impersonation)
  • Misinformation: Content deliberately designed to spread false information
  • Impersonation: Content falsely representing another person's identity

3.5 Adult Content

  • Pornography: Sexually explicit content (not allowed on our service)
  • Nudity: Graphic nudity or sexual content
  • Note: Artistic nudity in appropriate contexts (e.g., educational, medical) may be permitted at our discretion

3.6 Violent Content

  • Graphic violence, gore, or mutilation
  • Content glorifying violence or self-harm
  • Content promoting dangerous activities or challenges

4. Prohibited Uses

4.1 Copyright Infringement (DMCA)

  • Quota Evasion: Creating multiple accounts to bypass daily limits
