Can I Stream Subnautica 2?

Subnautica 2 runs on Unreal Engine 5 with Lumen lighting and Nanite geometry — it is one of the most demanding games to stream. Select your GPU below to see if you can handle it.

⚠️ Why Subnautica 2 Is Demanding

Unreal Engine 5's Lumen global illumination and Nanite geometry push GPUs hard. We recommend enabling DLSS Quality (NVIDIA) or FSR Quality (AMD/Intel) in-game, and always using hardware encoding (NVENC/AMF) in OBS — never x264 — to prevent OBS from competing with the game for resources.
