Can I Stream Subnautica 2 on NVIDIA GeForce RTX 3060?

✅ Good Performance

The RTX 3060's 12GB VRAM is a hidden advantage for Subnautica 2. Enable DLSS Quality, set Global Illumination to Medium, and enjoy 1080p streaming. The Ampere NVENC encoder is still excellent quality.
