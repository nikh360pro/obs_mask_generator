Can I Stream Dota 2 on a NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4070 Ti?

Verdict: Perfect ✅

The NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4070 Ti (NVENC encoder) handles Dota 2 at this tier. Very easy to stream. Top-down view requires moderate bitrate to prevent text blur.

Recommended OBS Settings

  • Encoder: NVENC (8th Gen (Ada))
  • Bitrate (1080p60): 6000 Kbps
  • Preset: P5
  • AV1 Support: Yes - Use it for YouTube!
