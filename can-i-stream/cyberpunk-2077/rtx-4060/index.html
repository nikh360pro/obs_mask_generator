Can I Stream Cyberpunk 2077 on a NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4060?

Verdict: Struggling ⚠️ (Use 720p)

The NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4060 (NVENC encoder) handles Cyberpunk 2077 at this tier. Extremely demanding on both CPU and GPU. Hardware encoding (NVENC/AMF) is mandatory to stream without stuttering.

Recommended OBS Settings

  • Encoder: NVENC (8th Gen (Ada))
  • Bitrate (1080p60): 6000 Kbps
  • Preset: P6
  • AV1 Support: Yes - Use it for YouTube!
