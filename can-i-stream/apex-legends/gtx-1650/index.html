Can I Stream Apex Legends on a NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1650?

Verdict: Struggling ⚠️ (Use 720p)

The NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1650 (NVENC encoder) handles Apex Legends at this tier. Fast-paced movement requires high bitrate (6000+ Kbps) to prevent pixelation during slides and jumps.

Recommended OBS Settings

  • Encoder: NVENC (6th Gen (Turing))
  • Bitrate (1080p60): 5000 Kbps
  • Preset: Quality
  • AV1 Support: No - Stick to H.264 or HEVC.
