Can I Stream Apex Legends on a NVIDIA GeForce RTX 3070?

Verdict: Perfect ✅

The NVIDIA GeForce RTX 3070 (NVENC encoder) handles Apex Legends at this tier. Fast-paced movement requires high bitrate (6000+ Kbps) to prevent pixelation during slides and jumps.

Recommended OBS Settings

  • Encoder: NVENC (7th Gen (Ampere))
  • Bitrate (1080p60): 6000 Kbps
  • Preset: P5
  • AV1 Support: No - Stick to H.264 or HEVC.
