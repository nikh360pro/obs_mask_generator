We do not collect personal information. Our tool runs entirely in your browser. No images, masks, or personal data are uploaded to any server.

Analytics

  • Referral source (how you found us)

This data is anonymous and aggregated. You can opt out using browser privacy settings or the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Analytics: Google Analytics uses cookies to track site usage
  • Advertising: If ads are displayed, third-party advertisers (like Google AdSense) may use cookies to show relevant ads

You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

Our site may use:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements (Google Ads Policy)

Data Security

Since our tool processes everything locally in your browser, your images and masks never leave your device. We have no access to your content.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
