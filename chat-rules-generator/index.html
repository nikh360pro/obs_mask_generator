Frequently Asked Questions

What chat rules should every Twitch channel have?

Essential Twitch chat rules include: Be respectful, no spam, no self-promotion without permission, no hate speech or discrimination, keep it family-friendly (if applicable), and listen to moderators.

How do I add chat rules to my Twitch channel?

Go to your Twitch Creator Dashboard ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Moderation ‚Üí Chat Rules. Paste your rules there and they'll be shown to viewers before they can chat.

How many chat rules should I have?

5-10 rules is ideal. Too few and you miss important guidelines, too many and viewers won't read them. Focus on the most important behaviors for your community.
