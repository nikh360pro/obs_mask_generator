Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose stream colors?

Pick a primary brand color you like, then use this tool to generate complementary colors. Stick to 3-5 colors max for a cohesive look.

What colors work best for streaming?

High-contrast colors work best. Use bright accent colors against dark backgrounds. Avoid colors that are too similar to green screen colors if you use one.
