Why Higher Bitrate on YouTube?

YouTube doesn't cap bitrate like Twitch. More bitrate = better quality. With AV1 encoding, you can achieve excellent quality at 9000 Kbps. With H.264, 12000 Kbps is recommended.

AV1 vs H.264 for YouTube

AV1 is preferred if you have RTX 40 series GPU. It provides ~40% better quality at the same bitrate. YouTube fully supports AV1 for live streaming.

FAQ

What bitrate for YouTube 1080p 60fps?

9000 Kbps with AV1, or 12000 Kbps with H.264. Both provide excellent quality.

Should I use AV1 for YouTube?

Yes, if your GPU supports it (RTX 40 series, RX 7000 series). AV1 is more efficient than H.264.
