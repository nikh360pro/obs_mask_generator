Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best bitrate for streaming on Twitch?

For Twitch, the maximum bitrate is 6000 Kbps. For 1080p 60fps gaming, use 6000 Kbps. For 720p 60fps, use 4500 Kbps. For 720p 30fps, 3000-4000 Kbps is sufficient.

What bitrate should I use for YouTube streaming?

YouTube allows higher bitrates than Twitch. For 1080p 60fps, use 4500-9000 Kbps. For 1440p 60fps, use 9000-18000 Kbps. For 4K 60fps, use 20000-51000 Kbps.

How do I calculate streaming bitrate?

Bitrate depends on resolution, FPS, and content motion. Higher resolution and FPS require more bitrate. Fast-paced games need higher bitrate than static content like Just Chatting.

What happens if my bitrate is too high?

If your bitrate exceeds your upload speed, you'll experience dropped frames and buffering. Also, Twitch has a 6000 Kbps cap - going higher wastes bandwidth.
