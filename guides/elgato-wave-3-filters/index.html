Best OBS Settings for Elgato Wave:3

Quick Answer: The Wave:3 has built-in "Clipguard". Enable this in the Wave Link software first. In OBS, do NOT use a Limiter, as Clipguard handles it better. Focus on EQ.

Wave Link vs. OBS Filters

You have a choice: run filters inside the Wave Link app (using VSTs) or inside OBS. Running them in Wave Link is better because it applies to Discord too, not just your stream.

Recommended Chain (VST or OBS)

  1. Noise Removal: Elgato Noise Removal (AI) is better than OBS RNNoise. Install it from the Elgato marketplace.
  2. EQ: Boost the lows slightly for presence.
  3. Compressor: Ratio 3:1.

Audio Out of Sync?

Wave Link adds about 20-50ms of latency. You might need to offset your camera to match.
