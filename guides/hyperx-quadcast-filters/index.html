Best OBS Filters for HyperX QuadCast

Quick Answer: The QuadCast is a condenser mic that sounds bright. To make it sound "fuller", use the 3-Band Equalizer to boost the Lows (+2dB) and a Compressor to even out volume.

Step 1: Polar Pattern

Ensure the dial on the back is set to the Cardioid symbol (Heart shape). This focuses on your voice and ignores your keyboard.

The Filter Chain

  1. Noise Suppression: RNNoise.
  2. 3-Band Equalizer: High: 0, Mid: -1, Low: +2 (Adds warmth/bass).
  3. Compressor: Ratio: 3:1, Threshold: -20dB, Output Gain: +4dB.
