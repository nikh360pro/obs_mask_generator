Best OBS Filters for Razer Seiren Mini

Quick Answer: This mic is "always on" (no physical mute/gain). It picks up desk vibrations easily. Use a Noise Gate heavily to cut out keyboard clacking.

Step 1: Mount It (Crucial)

Because it's so small, if you put it on your desk near your keyboard, it will sound like an earthquake every time you type. Put it on a cheap boom arm ($15) to isolate vibrations.

The Filter Chain

  1. Noise Suppression: RNNoise.
  2. Noise Gate: This is critical. Raise the "Close Threshold" to -28dB until your typing disappears when you aren't talking.
  3. Gain: +2dB (It's a bit quiet usually).
