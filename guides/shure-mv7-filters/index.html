Best OBS Settings for Shure MV7

Quick Answer: This dynamic mic is naturally quiet. You need Gain. Use the Compressor aggressively to keep volume consistent even if you move away from the mic.

Why it sounds quiet

The MV7 is a dynamic microphone (unlike the Yeti). It needs you to be very close (2-4 inches). If you lean back, volume drops effectively.

The Loudness Chain

  1. Gain: +3dB to +6dB (if maximizing the Shure app wasn't enough).
  2. Compressor: Ratio: 4:1, Threshold: -20dB, Attack: 2ms, Release: 100ms.
  3. Limiter: -2dB.

Audio Out of Sync?

USB mics often have different latency than webcams. Fix the delay easily.
