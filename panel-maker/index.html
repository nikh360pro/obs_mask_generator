Frequently Asked Questions

What size should Twitch panels be?

Twitch panels should be 320 pixels wide. The height is flexible (75-600 pixels), but 160 pixels is recommended for a clean 2:1 aspect ratio. File size must be under 2.9MB.

What panels should every Twitch channel have?

Essential Twitch panels include: About Me, Schedule, Social Links, Donate/Tips, Rules, Discord, and PC Specs. These help viewers learn about you and support your channel.

How do I add panels to my Twitch channel?

Go to your Twitch channel, scroll to the About section, click "Edit Panels", then click the + button to add a panel. Upload your image and add a title and link.

Can I create matching panels for my whole channel?

Yes! Simply keep the same style and colors selected, then change the panel type and text for each panel you need. This creates a cohesive look for your channel.
